White Lion

There is estimated to be fewer than 300 white lions in the world!

  • Habitat: Mostly zoos, Timbavati
  • Food Source: Hunting
  • Avg Life Span: 16.8 years
  • Earliest Sighting: 1938

White tigers are regarded as divine by locals in their home region of Timbavati. White lions are not albinos; the color is actually caused by a recessive trait. White lions color vary from blonde to near-white.
